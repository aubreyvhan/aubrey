THE FARM

#TRAVEL BUCKETLIST

One hour drive from the people's city, San Fernando to the land of the eagle of the North, Agoo. There is a restaurant that is truly an Instagram worthy, Budget friendly, travel bucketlist and food that taste like heaven. It is located at San Pedro, Agoo, La Union. The American-Ructic Country theme restaurant is really shouting a real beauty to the people who goes there.

In the entrance of the restaurant you can see there the "Santorini" type of signage that say's the name of the restaurant. By entering the place you can see the simple interior of the mini houses, they are divided. Upon entering the restaurant on the left side there is a pathway leading to the Garden Pavillion where anyone can book or celebrate their parties and special events like weddings and many more. Their Nipa Hut is an instagram worthy place we're antics is placed and shows aesthetically view. The Farm Potting Shed are one of the best part of this place because when you go inside or even outside of it the interior will give you chills because of its beauty, when you are inside of it you can feel the american breakfast vibe that you've dreamed of. The Farm Garden is also great for those who want to relax.

Interms of food, The Farm has many dishes to offer. their best selling product is the pata Tim and Barbeque. If you go there make sure that you should order and try this two dishes because they taste the best and too delicious to handle. Your money will be worth it when you go here because the foo is affordable and budget friendly.

I had so much fun when we went there. The crews are very accomodating and there are so many spots that is really instagram worthy that is why we takes so many pictures and we posted it in our social media sites.

The Farm Agoo is really astonishing in all terms. It promotes the beauty of having simple life at the farm.The ambiance is really good. The different parts of this restaurant has their differentkind of vibe that makes every traveler say "This is really a bucket list". Cioa!

GRUMPY JOE DAY

#CRAVING SATISFIED

After my college entrance examination, I went to Grumpy Joe with Stefano to have some brunch because I'm already hungry and also exhausted because the test is so hard. Its not my first time dining here, but I must say that their dishes is timeless. I ordered my favorite meal and it is the Combo B that consist of one buffalo wings,one serving of fries, a pizza and a delicious spaghetti.This combo will surely get your stomach satisfied and full. The flavors of each components really works together.I really love their buffalo wings because it has a balance taste that is really suited in the tongue. I also love how the spaghetti tasted because its not too sweet and it is not also too salty. We also ordered the Aloha 5 cheeze pizza because it is really my favorite in their list of dishes.

This resto has a welcoming aura which is really good and their crew is also accomodating and they always smile whenever a costumer will approach them. I love how they treat their costumers and they really welcomes you as their costumer.

Whenever I'm stressed I tend to eat a lot. Stress eating makes me fat but thats okay because whenever I eat my favorite foods, it makes me happy, yeah its weird but thats the truth and I love myself for being who I'am. Grumpy Joe really makes me happy.Ciao!
